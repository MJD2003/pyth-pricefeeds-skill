## Fee Structure

- **Update fee** is dynamic and chain-specific
- Always call `pyth.getUpdateFee(priceUpdate)` on-chain to get the current fee
- **Never hardcode fees**
- Fees are paid in native gas token (ETH, SOL, etc.)
- Fee is typically very small (< $0.01 per update on most chains)
